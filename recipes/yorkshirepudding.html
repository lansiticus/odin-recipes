Yorkshire Pudding

Description

Yorkshire pudding is a delicious and versatile English pastry that can be eaten at any meal with sweet or savory food. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 3/4 cup of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup of butter or lard

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, flour and salt. Do not overmix. Allow batter to rest at room teperature for 30 minutes
  3. Add a teaspoon of fat to each cup of a 12-cup muffin tin and transfer to the oven to heat, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Once hot, divide batter equally to fill the cups about halfway, and bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.
