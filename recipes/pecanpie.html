Pecan Pie

Description

Pecan trees are plentiful here in north Texas so pecan pie is a perennial holiday favorite. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup of pecans
  • 9 inch unbaked pie shell
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups of white sugar
  • 1/4 cup of dark corn syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of cold water
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix cold water and cornstarch into a slurry
  3. Combine cornstarch slurry with sugar, corn syrup and butter in a saucepan over medium heat until a full boil is reached. Remove from heat.
  4. In a large bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Gradually beat in cooked syrup mixture.
  5. Stir in salt, vanilla and pecans.
  6. Pour into pie shell and bake until filling is set, 45-50 minutes
